2. Use of TrueDepth API and Facial Data

Our App, FlashIQ, uses the TrueDepth API to enhance the user experience by enabling certain features that require facial recognition. The TrueDepth API is designed to provide the highest level of security and privacy for your data.

What Data Do We Collect?

We may use the TrueDepth API to capture and process facial data to enable features such as face tracking and facial expression recognition. This data is used only to provide these features within the App and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

How Is Your Facial Data Processed?

The facial data captured by the TrueDepth API is processed in real-time on your device and is not stored or transmitted to any external servers. The data is used solely to provide the specific features within the App, such as displaying flashcards in Augmented Reality (AR).

Why Do We Use Your Facial Data?

The facial data is used exclusively to enable and enhance App features like AR, allowing you to view your study cards in the real world and to improve your interaction with the App. This data is processed securely and remains on your device at all times.

Security and Privacy Measures

We take your privacy seriously and have implemented strict security measures to protect your data. The facial data captured by the TrueDepth API is encrypted and processed only on your device. We do not store, share, or transmit this data outside of your device.

User Control

You have full control over the use of facial data within our App. If you choose to disable features that use the TrueDepth API, you can do so at any time in the App's settings.

By using FlashIQ, you consent to the processing of your facial data as described in this section. If you have any concerns or questions regarding the use of your facial data, please contact us at hello@bytesavvy.com.
